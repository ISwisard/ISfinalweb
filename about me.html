About Me

This is what I do.

I was born January 13, 2000 in Coppell, Texas. When I was 6 years old my family moved to the near-by city of Southlake, and we have lived there ever sense. I have one older brother named Jacob and two older sisters named Lexie and Sarah. All of them are married which makes me the last unmarried kid in my family. Being the youngest has its benefits, but I defiantly cant wait to get married so I’m not without a partner when we play family games. I have two amazing parents who have taught me so much and have helped me become the person I am today. I grew up playing many sports but eventually dialed in on two: basketball and swimming, which I played competitively throughout my high school days. I have always loved anything and everything that can keep me active, but I also enjoy card games, puzzles, movies, and a good nap. Family and friends are very important to me and I have been blessed with the best of both. Above all else, I am a member of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints and daily strive to be a disciple of Jesus Christ. I have a strong faith in Him, which brought me to serve a two-year mission in great city of Bogota Colombia. My faith is also the core influence for my desire to be a great husband and father one day.
